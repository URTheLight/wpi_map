About This Map

The heat map uses data from World Labor Group, aiming to visualize historical labor actions.

About z-score & Visualized Index

The z-score of each country is calculated with its historical mean and standard deviation of the country. The equation used is z-score = (case of current year- average cases)/standard deviation of historical cases.

This means that each country's z-score indicates how current year's labor actions compare to the country's own historical data.

The visualized index is z-score multiply by 100, for color coding purpose only.
